The Estate

Nestled along one of Sri Lanka's most exquisite north-western beaches — a haven of tranquility.

With kilometres of soft, golden sand stretching along the shimmering ocean, and inviting waters that maintain a pleasant 28–30 °C year-round, the coastline in front of the villa is the perfect setting for swimming, kitesurfing, and watching dolphins in their natural habitat.

Located just two hours from Colombo's international airport, Dolphin View Estate is easily accessible — while offering a peaceful tropical retreat.

Architecture

Harmony through symmetry, clean lines, and the golden ratio.

The sophisticated architecture and design create harmony through symmetry, clean lines, and the Greek golden ratio — high-vaulted ceilings, elegant teakwood floors, sculpted columns, and intricate wooden window grids. Walls of sparkling white natural stone, each set by hand, and the graceful curves of Roman tiles on the roof accentuate the serenity of the building.

Living area

Generously proportioned, and open to the sea.

The living and dining areas sit at the centre of the villa, beneath the vaulted teak ceiling, opening to an island kitchen and 180° of ocean.

The Suites

Two suites — Sapphire and Ruby.

Both well-appointed, each with an en-suite bathroom and a large veranda across two floors, each able to host a family of four.

Pool & Shore

A 17-metre infinity pool, facing the beach.

Tucked away on a 2.5-acre estate with over a hundred swaying coconut palms, the villa and its seamlessly integrated 17-metre infinity pool face the beach — with spectacular panoramic ocean views and breathtaking sunsets. A few steps through the grove open onto the sand, and your own stretch of shore.

The Setting

The water, the wild, and the ancient.

A rare stretch of coast where a single day can hold flat-water kiting at dawn, leopards by mid-morning, and a thousand-year-old city by afternoon — all within a scenic drive of the estate.

Water

On the water.

Wave-riding and kitesurfing are possible directly in front of the estate, and the waters stay a swimmable 28–30 °C year-round. For flatwater conditions, the Kappalady lagoon is about 30 minutes away and the Kalpitiya lagoon 45 minutes — together, some of the finest kiting in Asia. Offshore, dolphins pass in their hundreds.

Nature

In the wild.

The renowned Wilpattu and Minneriya National Parks — home to leopards and large elephant gatherings — and the untouched Ritigala Strict Natural Reserve, with the remnants of a lost city, immerse you in pristine nature.

Culture

Ancient wonders.

The ancient marvels of Anuradhapura, Sigiriya, Polonnaruwa, and Dambulla promise unforgettable cultural experiences — ruined royal cities, rock fortresses, and cave temples, each a UNESCO World Heritage site within a scenic drive.

Ownership & Opportunity

Yours to own, and to grow.

Dolphin View comes with a clean, well-established ownership structure suited to international buyers. We're glad to walk qualified purchasers through the details in private.

Ideal as a luxury residence, it can equally serve as a vacation home paired with a high-end rental business.
